Cthulhu Shirt Design

A while ago, I got the idea to finally start designing some tees. When one of my teachers informed me about a competition online, I couldn't refuse to give it a shot. The contest was on Teeville, a nice initiative that unfortunately doesn't exist anymore, where you could submit a design, which was then reviewed by professionals and afterwards you could vote on other designs. If your tshirt got picked out, you received your design on a tshirt and other people could order one from the webshop. I tried to draw a Cthulhu figure because yeah you know, internet and stuff. Hope you like it!
